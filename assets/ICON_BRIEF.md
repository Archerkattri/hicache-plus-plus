# hicache-pp (HiCache++) — icon brief (for image generation)

A minimalist, modern logo for **hicache-pp**, a training-free accelerator that speeds up diffusion and flow-matching inference by forecasting cached steps instead of re-running the network. Combine a cache/layers motif (a few stacked rounded bars) with a strong sense of speed — a lightning bolt or fast-forward arrow cutting through the layers, or motion lines suggesting skipped steps. Use an energetic palette (electric violet or orange over a dark base) on a transparent background, in a flat geometric vector style that feels sharp and dynamic. Keep it clean at small sizes; it should feel fast and technical, like an ML inference-speed tool.

Deliverables: a 512×512 transparent-PNG logo (square) and a 1280×640 PNG social-preview banner.
